## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
We do **not** collect any personal information directly from you:
-  No account creation required
-  No name, email, or contact information requested
-  No user-generated content collected

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

#### **Quiz Progress (Local Only)**
- Your quiz scores and chapter progress are stored **locally on your device**
- This data **never leaves your device**
- You can reset this data anytime in Settings

#### **Advertising Data (Third-Party - Google AdMob)**
AmpedUp displays advertisements provided by **Google AdMob**. Google may collect:
- **Device identifiers** (Advertising ID, Android ID)
- **IP address** (approximate location)
- **Device information** (model, OS version, screen size)
- **App usage patterns** (pages viewed, ad interactions)
- **Approximate location** (based on IP address)

**Purpose:** To display relevant advertisements and measure ad performance.

**Your Control:** You can opt out of personalized ads:
- **Android:** Settings ? Google ? Ads ? Opt out of Ads Personalization
- **Learn more:** https://support.google.com/ads/answer/2662922

#### **Crash Reports (Optional, Local-Only)**
We may store minimal, anonymized crash logs locally on your device to help diagnose issues:
- Device model and OS version
- App version and crash stack traces
- **No personal information**
- **Stored locally only** (not transmitted to our servers)
- **Optional:** Can be disabled in Settings
- **Can be cleared:** App Diagnostics ? Clear Logs

## 4. Your Rights (GDPR - EU/Czech Republic)

If you are in the European Union (including Czech Republic), you have the right to:

-  **Access:** Request what data we have (none, as we don't store personal data)
-  **Deletion:** Request data deletion (quiz progress can be reset in Settings)
-  **Opt-Out:** Disable personalized ads via Android settings
-  **Portability:** Export your data (quiz progress can be reset and retaken)
-  **Object:** Object to data processing (opt out of personalized ads)

**To exercise these rights:**
- **Quiz Progress:** Settings ? Reset Progress
- **Ads:** Android Settings ? Google ? Ads ? Opt out of Ads Personalization
- **Other Requests:** Email info@benholland.net

## 5. Data Security

### **Local Data:**
- Quiz progress is stored in your device's secure app storage
- Accessible only by AmpedUp
- Protected by your device's security (PIN, fingerprint, etc.)

### **No Server Storage:**
- We do not maintain user databases
- No quiz data is transmitted to our servers
- No risk of data breaches from our side

### **Third-Party Security:**
- Google AdMob uses industry-standard encryption (HTTPS)
- Google Play Billing uses secure payment processing

## 6. Children's Privacy

AmpedUp is suitable for ages **13+**. 

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Google AdMob's policies also restrict ads shown to children.

If you believe a child under 13 has used the app, please contact us at info@benholland.net.

**Parents:** You can control ad personalization for child accounts via Google Family Link.
